About Facebook

Facebook Pages are public spaces. Anyone who can see the Page can see your post or comment. When you post or comment on a Page, a story can be published in Feed and other places on Facebook. You can only post on Pages that have allowed visitor posts. To post on a Page that you visit:


1.From your Feed, click Search Facebook in the top left.
2.Search for the Page you'd like to post on, then select it from the drop-down menu.
3.Click Create post at the top of the Page and write your post.
4.Click Post.
